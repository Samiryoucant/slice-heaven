Our Story

Founded in 2010 by master pizzaiolo Giovanni Rossi, Slice Heaven began as a small family-owned pizzeria in Naples, Italy. Giovanni brought his generations-old family recipes and techniques to America, combining authentic Italian tradition with innovative new flavors.

Today, we continue Giovanni's legacy by crafting each pizza with the same passion and attention to detail that made his original pizzeria famous. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauce is simmered for hours to develop deep flavor, and our toppings are always the freshest available.
